About me


My name is Jesús Eduardo Palomo Delgado.

I’m Eduardo, a passionate and self-taught 3D artist with over 8 years of experience in modeling, texturing, and environment creation for games and interactive media. Since I was 16, I’ve been honing my skills in various areas of 3D art, including UV/UDIM mapping, stylized and PBR texturing, and real-time integration using Unreal Engine and Unity.I’ve contributed to several independent projects and worked professionally I’m proficient with tools like Blender, Substance Painter, Marmoset Toolbag, and familiar with programming languages such as C# to improve workflows.


I’m always eager to learn and grow, currently expanding my knowledge in programming and automation to complement my artistic skills. I’m looking to connect with other professionals and creative teams to collaborate on exciting projects in the videogame and XR/VR industries.
